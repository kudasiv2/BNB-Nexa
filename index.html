Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about BNBNexa

What is the minimum investment?

The minimum investment is 0.01 BNB. You can start with this small amount and reinvest your profits to grow your portfolio exponentially over time.

How much can I earn daily?

You can earn 2.5% daily on your investment for 50 days, which totals 125% return on your initial deposit. This means if you invest 1 BNB, you'll receive 1.25 BNB in total over 50 days.

How does the referral system work?

Our referral program has 5 levels: 10% for level 1 (direct referrals), 5% for level 2, 3% for level 3, and 1% for levels 4 and 5. You earn commissions from your direct referrals and their referrals up to 5 levels deep, creating a powerful passive income stream.

Is there a withdrawal fee?

There are no withdrawal fees for ROI earnings. A 10% fee is applied only to the initial deposit which goes to the development and maintenance of the platform, ensuring its long-term sustainability.

Is the contract audited and secure?

Yes, our smart contract is verified on BscScan and fully transparent. The contract has no owner privileges, making it truly decentralized and secure. You can view the source code and verify its security yourself on BscScan.
