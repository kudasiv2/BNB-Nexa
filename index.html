Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment is 0.01 BNB. You can invest any amount above this minimum.
How much can I earn daily?
You earn 2.5% daily ROI on your deposited amount for 50 days, totaling 125% return on investment.
How does the referral system work?
You earn commissions from 5 levels: 10% from level 1, 5% from level 2, 3% from level 3, and 1% from levels 4 & 5.
When can I withdraw my earnings?
You can withdraw ROI rewards anytime, and referral rewards can be claimed as they accumulate. There's no minimum withdrawal amount.
Is my investment secure?
Yes, all transactions are secured by Binance Smart Chain technology and recorded on the blockchain for full transparency.
