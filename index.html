How to Join BNBNexa

1. Connect Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button and authorize the connection with your MetaMask wallet. Ensure you're on Binance Smart Chain network.

2. Make Your Deposit

Choose your investment plan and deposit any amount from 0.01 BNB. Higher deposits get better daily returns.

3. Earn Daily Returns

Start earning daily ROI from 1% to 1.5% based on your investment tier. Returns are calculated and available for withdrawal daily.

4. Withdraw Anytime

Withdraw your daily ROI earnings and referral bonuses anytime. No lock-in period for profits.

5. Earn Referral Bonuses

Share your referral link and earn 5% on level 1, 3% on level 2, and 1% on levels 3-4 from your team's investments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BNBNexa? +

BNBNexa is a decentralized investment platform on Binance Smart Chain offering daily returns through smart contract-based farming.

What is the minimum investment? +

The minimum investment is 0.01 BNB.

How are daily returns calculated? +

Returns are calculated based on your investment tier: 1% for 0.01-1 BNB, 1.2% for 1-5 BNB, and 1.5% for 5+ BNB.

When can I withdraw my earnings? +

You can withdraw ROI earnings once per day and referral bonuses anytime.

Is there a referral program? +

Yes! Earn 5% on level 1, 3% on level 2, and 1% on levels 3-4 from your referrals.

What network should I use? +

BNBNexa operates exclusively on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Make sure your wallet is connected to BSC Mainnet.

Are there any fees? +

There's a 10% owner fee taken from the TVL, which doesn't affect your investment amount.

How long does the investment last? +

The investment plan runs for 120 days with daily returns.

Is the smart contract secure? +

Yes, the contract is decentralized with no owner control over user funds. View it on BSCScan for verification.

What happens after 120 days? +

After 120 days, you stop earning daily ROI but can continue with referral earnings. You can reinvest anytime.

Security & Smart Contract
